Export

In International Trade the term ‘export’ is used to mean “selling of services from home country to other markets”. However according to the Export of Service rules, any taxable service shall be treated as export of service “if such service is provided from India and used outside India”[2]. The Delhi tribunal in the case of Microsoft Corpn (i) (P) Ltd. v.
